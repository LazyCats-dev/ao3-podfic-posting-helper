AO3 Podfic Posting Helper

Navigate to the "New Work" page on AO3, fill in the URL of the fic you were inspired by in this pop-up, and watch all the associated metadata get imported for you! You can even set custom default work text by going to this extension's options.

Note: this extension assumes it's dealing with an empty form. Anything you enter in the form before importing metadata may get erased!
